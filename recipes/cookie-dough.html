Edible Cookie Dough

Description

This edible cookie dough recipe is egg-free and will satisfy any cravings for chocolate chip cookies without the wait.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Brown sugar
  • Butter
  • Vanilla
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Heat-treat the flour in the microwave.
  2. Combine the ingredients to form a crumbly dough.
